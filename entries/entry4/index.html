In fields of green,

the clover lies,

A lucky charm before our eyes.

With emerald hues, it graces land,

In nature's care, it takes its stand.



Four leaves it bears, a rare delight,

A symbol of luck, from day to night.

In meadows lush, where secrets hide,

The green of fortune, far and wide.



So cherish this, the verdant hue,

sign of hope, forever true.

In every shade, in every place,

Green brings luck and endless grace.
