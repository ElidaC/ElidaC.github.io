    Psychological Aspects of BLUE    

  1.     Calming and Peace: Blue makes people feel calm and relaxed, which is why it's used in places like bedrooms and spas.
  2.     Trustworthy: Blue symbolizes trust and reliability. Many businesses use it to gain people's confidence.
  3.     Traditional and Safe: Blue is a common, safe choice for traditional settings.
  4.     Emotions like Sadness: Blue can sometimes make us feel sad or distant, depending on the situation.
  5.     Creativity Blue can help us work better and be more creative, so some offices use it in their design.
  6.     Relaxing Physically: Blue can physically relax us by slowing our heart rate and cooling our bodies down.
